The Long Count

Reckon the nights between your embrace and the age you're reaching for — rest and Velarium included.

Natural nights assumes one night of age per real night since your embrace. Enter your character's actual current age to capture whatever rest and Velarium have already earned you — the calculator works out the difference on its own. From tonight forward it assumes two extra nights for every week you rest, plus one for every ten Velarium spent. Your own pace may vary — adjust the figures above as your habits become clearer. Age tiers are drawn from the DawnDream wiki. Nothing entered here is saved or sent anywhere — it lives only in your browser.
